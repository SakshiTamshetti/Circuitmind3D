About CircuitMind 3D

We build tools that make complex scientific concepts visually intuitive and AI-explainable for everyone.

Our Mission

CircuitMind 3D exists to close the gap between abstract scientific knowledge and genuine understanding. Seeing something in three dimensions — and being able to ask questions about it in plain language — transforms how people learn.

What We Build

Our platform combines high-fidelity 3D models of scientific subjects with real-time AI explanation. Every component is annotated, every question is answerable, and every model is interactive.

Technology

We use WebGL and Three.js for rendering, a custom annotation layer for component metadata, and large language models for contextual explanation. Our architecture is open and designed for easy integration with custom ML models.
